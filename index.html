  • The daily benefits of one glass of milk

    Milk is an easily digestible source of calcium. And that's the pure truth. Easy to digest fat and vitamin D, which is also found in dairy products, contribute to the absorption of calcium and the correct distribution of this element in the body.

    Milk protein contains all the amino acids necessary for the human body — tiny "bricks" that make up all proteins. There are 20 main varieties of amino acids, 8 of which are indispensable, that is, they cannot be formed in the body, but must be obtained from food. Euphorbia protein is able to fully compensate for the need for all amino acids.

    • 35% Riboflavin
    • 25% Calcium
    • 20% Phosphorus
    • 20% Vitamin B5
    • 15% Vitamin A
    • 15% Vitamin D
    • 8% Potassium
    • 10% Niacin
